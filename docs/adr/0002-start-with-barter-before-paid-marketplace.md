# ADR 0002: Start With Barter Before Paid Marketplace

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

A paid storage marketplace is attractive, but it introduces fraud, disputes, refunds, payout compliance, tax issues, illegal content complaints, provider reliability claims, and higher user expectations.

## Decision

Start with invite-only reciprocal or credit-based barter. Delay paid marketplace features until technical reliability and operational controls are proven.

## Consequences

Positive:

- Lower legal and operational pressure.
- Easier trust building in homelab communities.
- Faster learning cycle.
- Less support burden from payment disputes.

Negative:

- Slower monetization.
- Matching must handle fairness without money.
- Users who only want to buy storage may wait.

## Paid Marketplace Preconditions

- Restore reliability demonstrated.
- Peer replacement tested.
- Abuse process documented.
- Legal review completed.
- Payment and payout provider selected.
- Provider reliability controls in place.
